STARTUP BRANDING AND MARKETING 

It has been a saying “If you can’t tell anything, you can’t sell anything”.

Through the platform of Vadodara Start-up Festival 2020, the start-ups will get an opportunity to highlight their work with an effective marketing strategy, which will help to increase the brand value of their start-up. The Start-ups can submit material related to their work which will be showcased in the social media groups managed by Vadodara Start-up Festival and Parul University.
