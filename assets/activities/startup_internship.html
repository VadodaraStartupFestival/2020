START-UP INTERN CARNIVAL 

An opportunity for the students and the VSF20 participants to get internships in India’s top notch start-ups. Working with a start-up will get the students a great exposure to their talent. Even the Start-ups will get an advantage to foster their growth with the young minds containing innovative ideas. Selected students will get the offer letter on the same day from the start-ups.
