INVESTOR- STARTUP CONNECT (PITCHING SESSIONS) 

“If there is something that stands out about the product, try starting your pitch with that”.

A pitching session will be of five to ten minute meeting with investors during which innovators can "pitch" their work in front of investors. This helps in getting the investor’s interest and attention immediately. The pitching session has been divided into two different phases based on potential of the start-ups i.e.

A.Ideation

In this stage, you have a concept but you do not yet have a business. You may have not yet even developed your product. What you have is an idea for a great product or service and an inkling of how you could form a business around it. During this stage, you spend lots of time pitching your idea and asking for opinions from friends, colleagues and industry contacts to get a feel for your potential market and to gauge the viability of turning your inspiration into reality.
Vadodara Start-up Festival will provide a platform for innovators where they can grab the opportunity to pitch their idea/concept of project in front of private and government investors.This will further help in:

  • Providing funds up to two lakhs for POC (Proof of Concept) development.
  • Providing connections to innovators for their further development.
  • Providing the IPR support for selected and required ideas.
  • Providing mentoring opportunities from Industry Partners.

B.Development phase

This phase include the start-ups which have been developed into a business model. This further have three stages:

  • Early stage – In the early stage, aspects of the company remain incomplete, although there is usually evidence of progress in the company’s development.Typically, the management team is incomplete, the product or service is still in development, or if developed is in the testing stage and has not yet gone to market on a commercialized basis. If the product is commercially available, it is generating revenue on a very limited basis.

  • Growth Stage – During the growth phase, the company should have a stable customer base. It should be consistently generating income and attracting more customers that are new. Consistent revenue generation stabilizes cash flow, which can be used to cover recurring expenses.

  • Acceleration stage – In this stage, the business must venture into new markets by expanding products or service offerings, moving beyond the region or opening new locations. This rapidly increases the rate at which a company grows.This stage and its rapid expansion present several new challenges to business owners who might suddenly find themselves facing fresh cash flow challenges with their resources and time stretched thinner than ever before. Owners of this stage have several options for moving forward. They can attempt to maintain the company as it is; they can consider whether or not further expansion is possible, smart and financially feasible; or they can consider exiting the company, either by handing the reins over to another individual or selling it completely.
    Through the platform of Vadodara Start-up Festival, the Start-ups of development stage will get an opportunity to interact with investors and industry people and pitch about their work for funding or expansion.The VSF platform will be:

    • Providing connections to innovators for their further development.
    • Providing mentoring opportunities from Industry Partners.
    • Providing the funding platform with investors.

    Note: The best Start-up pitch in development phase have an opportunity to grab a price money of 25,000 rupees.
